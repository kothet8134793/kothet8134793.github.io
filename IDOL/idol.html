Elon Musk's career began with co-founding Zip2, which was sold for $300 million in 1999. He then founded X.com, which became PayPal and was sold to eBay for $1.5 billion in 2002. In 2002, he founded SpaceX with the goal of reducing space travel costs and enabling Mars colonization. In 2004, Musk joined Tesla as CEO, leading it to become a leader in electric vehicles. He also founded SolarCity, The Boring Company, Neuralink, and co-founded OpenAI. Musk is known for his innovations in space, energy, and AI.
